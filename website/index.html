Machine Learning Scoring Predictions and The Sportsbook Market

What is the Best Way to Predict Future Scores Based on Past Data?

The purpose of this project was to analyze several different weather variables and their relationship to latitude. We looked at the maximum temperature, humidity, cloudiness and wind speed from a random sample of approximately 550 cities across the globe to see how position relative to the equator has on weather desirabilty. We used the citipy module to generate our sample cities and the OpenWeatherMap API to gather our weath data on each city. Overall, we found that as cities move further away from the equator, the more their maximum temperature drops. All other weather events we looked at had weak relationships to latitude. Weather continues to be a highly variable process influenced by a multitude of different variables.

One note to keep in mind: Since we chose to use cities as our markers for our weather variables, we end up getting more Northern Hemisphere cities than Southern Hemisphere ones. This is due to more hospitable land mass being available in this Hemisphere and cities providing continually updated data.
